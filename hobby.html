Hobbies

When I'm not developing the web, I like to unwind with a few hobbies that keep me balanced and entertained.
I spend a lot of time sketching futuristic cityscapes, blending architecture and sci-fi elements as a fun way to
stretch my creativity beyond code. I also enjoy experimenting in the kitchen, often trying to recreate dishes
from different cultures — with varying levels of success, but always with enthusiasm. And when I really need to
reset, I compete in online rubber duck beauty contests — a deeply unserious but fiercely competitive corner of the
internet where presentation is everything.
